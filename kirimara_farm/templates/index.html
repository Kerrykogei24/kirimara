Our Story

Description

Kirimara Estate lies in the foothills of Mount Kenya and The Aberdare Mountain Range. Bordering River Honi and River Chania. The Estate spans an impressive 120 hectares of prime coffee. It benefits from Nyeri`s rich,fertile volcanic soils ,coupled with a climate that provides ideal temperatures and consistent rainfall. While many farms have transitioned to newer cultivars like Ruiru 11,Kirimara continues to grow the classic SL28 & SL34 varieties . Kirimara Estate has long been celebrated for its AA Kenya coffee-a grade synonymous with superior quality.

Our Mission

To cultivate premium ,sustainably grown coffee that reflects our passion for quality,our respect for the environment,empower our local community and delight coffee lovers with authentic traceable quality from seed to cup.

Our Vision

To be a leading coffee estate recognized globally for excellence in every aspect of our work. We envision a future where our estate stands as a symbol of quality ,integrity and innovation in the global coffee industry. Protecting the environment through regenerative agricultural practices and resource stewardship. Empowering our local community through fair employment,education and shared prosperity. Continually innovating, while preserving the cultural and natural heritage of our land.
