What We Do

Kirimara Coffee Estate is dedicated to producing exceptional coffee while maintaining a deep commitment to sustainability and community development. From hand-picking the finest cherries to carefully drying each bean, every stage of the coffee harvesting process is performed with care and tradition. Beyond coffee production, the estate is deeply involved in uplifting the local community, providing employment, training, and supporting youth empowerment programs.
